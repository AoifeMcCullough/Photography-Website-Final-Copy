About Jane

Jane is now firmly established as one of Britains leading landscape and people photographers.

She was born in 1986 and worked in British Theatre and Television for 5 years. Throughout this perios she became fascinated by theatrical lighting and design. Gradually the landscape and the way it can be revealed to us through light and shade lead her to develop her interests in landscape photography.

Following on from this she also became interested in photographing people in the enviroment around them which lead to a number of awards and high profile commissions.
