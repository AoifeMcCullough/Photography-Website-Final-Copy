Photography

I have a passionate interest in Photography. Over the last tenyears my intrerest in photography has grown and I have attended many courses to improve my technical knowledge. My interests are landscapes and people

Production

I have a range of experience in pre and post production and have developed a number of techniques that I like to use and experiment with to ensure that my clients get unique photographs.

Montage

This is the technique of making a composite photography by using image-editing software. This technique is referred to as "Composting", and in casual usage is often called "photoshopping" (from the name of the most popular software). This is becoming more popular with my clients.
